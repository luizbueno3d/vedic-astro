How To Read This

Dasha means planetary time period. In simple language, this page shows which planet is running your life chapter now. Mahadasha is the main chapter, Antardasha is the sub-chapter, and Pratyantardasha is the smaller active trigger. If you want to understand what is unfolding right now, start with the currently active periods before looking at future timelines.

This page now shows two timing systems: the standard Vimshottari dasha below, and a Jaimini Chara Dasha section further down. Vimshottari is planet-based. Chara Dasha is sign-based.

Jaimini Chara Dasha

Chara Dasha is a sign-based timing system from Jaimini astrology. Instead of planets running the periods, the signs themselves run them. This app is using a clearly labeled conservative method: start from the Ascendant sign, move forward for odd signs and backward for even signs, then measure each sign's duration by counting to the sign where its ruler sits.
