What These Charts Are

Divisional charts are support charts. They do not replace the birth chart; they refine it. A simple way to think about them: the D1 chart gives the main life structure, and these charts zoom in on specific topics. For example, D9 is often used for marriage and inner maturity, D10 for career, and D7 for children and creative lineage.
