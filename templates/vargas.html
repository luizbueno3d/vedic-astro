Divisional Charts — {{ chart.name }}

Divisional charts help you zoom into specific life topics. We now separate the trusted core charts from advanced or still-verifying ones.

What These Charts Are

Divisional chartsi are support charts. They do not replace the birth chart; they refine it. A simple way to think about them: the D1 chart gives the main life structure, and these charts zoom in on specific topics. For example, D9i is often used for marriage and inner maturity, D10i for career, and D7i for children and creative lineage.

How To Read This Page

First trust the birth chart (D1). Then use D9 and D10 as the main confirmation charts. The topic charts lower on the page are useful, but only for specific questions. Some higher vargas are intentionally hidden until their formulas are individually verified.
