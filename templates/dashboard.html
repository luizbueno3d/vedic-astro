D1 — Rashi Chart (North Indian)

Start here first. This is the base chart of the whole reading: your sky map at birth. In plain English, it shows the main structure of your life. The Ascendant and its ruler are usually the first things to judge, because they describe the person carrying the whole chart.

KP Bhava Chalit — Nakshatra Nadi System

House placement based on sub-lord of nakshatra pada — the system Babajeet uses for real Dasha results.

Read this after the D1 chart, not before it. The D1 chart shows the natal setup; this BCC / KP layeri shows where results tend to land in real life, especially during dashas. So a planet keeps its natal nature, but its lived manifestation may shift houses here.
